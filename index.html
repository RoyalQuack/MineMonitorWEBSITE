What is MineMonitor?

MineMonitor is your ultimate Discord companion for tracking and managing Minecraft mod updates. Stay ahead of the game with real-time notifications about your favorite mods and modpacks.

For Developers

MineMonitor empowers mod developers with powerful tools to keep their community engaged. Our webhook integration system allows you to automatically notify your users about new releases, ensuring your updates reach your audience instantly. With our comprehensive API and automated version tracking, you can focus on development while we handle update notifications and community engagement.

For Players

Never miss an update for your favorite mods again! MineMonitor simplifies the mod management experience by providing real-time notifications directly in your Discord server. Stay informed about version updates, compatibility changes, and new features with our customizable notification system. Whether you're running a single mod or managing a complex modpack, MineMonitor keeps you in the loop with detailed changelogs and seamless update tracking.
